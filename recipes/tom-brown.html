SHITO RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of yellow corn
  • 1 cup of millet
  • 1 n half cup of soya beans
  • 1 cup of groundnut
  • 2fingers of unripe plantain
  • A handful of crayfish
  • 2 cups water

Directions

  1. Dissolve the powder in water
  2. Put it on fire( low heat)
  3. Stir continuously to avoid lumps, until your desired thickness is achieved leaving room for milk. dissolve any age appropriate milk add to it and serve,as an adult you can can sugar or sweetener,enjoy!
